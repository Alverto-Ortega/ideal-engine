Quick facts about Bill Gates' achievements:

  • Mr.Gates with the help of Colleague Allen, founded Microsoft in 1976.
  • On Novemeber 20,1985 the Microsoft company introduced an operating enivornment named "Windows".
  • In 1986, the month of March was the beggining of another achievement. Microsoft held an initial public offering of 2.5 million shares in which by the end of that year Gates had become a billionaire, at age 31!
  • Not surprisingly, Bill Gates became the richest man on the planet at the age of 39, remaining number one on the Forbes list of the World's Richest People until 2007.
  • Bill gates and his wife Melinda formed the "Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation" in the year 2000.
  • In 2010, Gates signed the "Gates-Buffet Giving Pledge", a generous donation.
